Why Join The Bootcamp?

DESIGN THINKING APPROACH​

We believe that the main aim of learning to code is to solve problems, so we take our students through the five-step design thinking process – Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test.

PROJECT-BASED LEARNING

Our lessons connect what our students learn to real-world scenarios. Students learn with the same approach they will eventually use in their hobbies, passions, and careers.
